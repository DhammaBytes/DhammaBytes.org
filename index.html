DhammaTalks Mobile

iOS application that lets you listen to the daily morning and evening talks given at Wat Metta Monastery by Than Ajaan Geoff (Thanissaro Bhikkhu). It brings talk series, chants in Pāli, guided meditations, and lectures to your iPhone or iPad. Most of the talks available on DhammaTalks.org are accessible through this app.

Access To Insight

Entire offline version of John Bullitt's widely recognized Access to Insight website, a massive compilation of material about Theravāda Buddhism and Buddhism in general. To aid the reader, indexes are available by subject, author, title, sutta title, proper name of historical figure, simile/metaphor, and even traditional numerical association.

Pāli Practice

Desktop & mobile application for learning Pāli language grammar, with a focus on verb conjugations and noun declensions. It uses flashcards with spaced repetition for better memorization. Works fully offline and is designed for brief, regular study, but does not replace textbooks, courses, or teachers. Word and grammar data are sourced from the Digital Pāli Dictionary project.
